Helping Serbian Families with Children in Kosovo

Restoring hope, one family at a time.

Deca Kosova is a Canadian humanitarian initiative focused on practical, transparent aid for Serbian families and children living in Kosovo.

Learn More

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect directly with Serbian families in Kosovo, understand their needs, and provide meaningful support such as groceries, school supplies, clothing, household essentials, and basic appliances.

Why We Started

Deca Kosova began with a simple belief: help should be personal, direct, and transparent.

Our goal is to connect with trusted local contacts in Kosovo, learn about families with children who need support, and provide practical assistance that makes a real difference in daily life.

Who We Help

👨‍👩‍👧 Families

Serbian families facing financial hardship while raising children in Kosovo.

🧒 Children

Children who need school supplies, clothing, food, or other everyday essentials.

🏠 Practical Needs

Groceries, appliances, heating, repairs, and other necessities that improve daily life.

How We Help

🛒 Groceries

Food and basic household supplies for families with children.

🎒 School Needs

Backpacks, notebooks, clothing, shoes, and supplies for children.

🏠 Household Support

Appliances, repairs, heating help, and other practical family needs.
